Code of Conduct

PVP is encouraged, but, it is easy to completely ruin the game for other players.
For this reason we are strongly against obvious greifing.
In general griefing is defined as intentionally hurting or harrasing players with the goal of making
them quit the game. For example: You knockout, hadcuff, and torture a beginning player for a long amount of time.
This would result in a serious warning if not an immediate ban.
Other offenses can be difined as: Modding (aim-bot & ect), Illegal Acts,
Threating Real Life Actions, Completely Wiping Bases For No Obvious Reason, and ect.
Also, your steam name cannot be '123' or anything unreadable.

If you encounter any form of code violation please
take digital evidence and reachout to the admin on the server (Under the tribe name 'Cream Team').
We love to PVP and hope everyone can do it responsibly and respectably.
